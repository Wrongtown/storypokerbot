Story planning poker in slack for your agile team

Storypoker makes it easy to point stories for your team.

Get Started Quickly

Minimal Setup needed. Just click on Add to Slack and Start immediately.

Built for Agile

Uses agile estimating and planning technique Like Fibonacci numbers in Golden ratios. Each estimator privately estimates his or her estimate. All estimates are revealed at the same time.

Minimal

Storypoker is simple & minimal. You can point again and again then repoint or dismiss.

Plays Nice

Storypoker lives in Slack. Its great for remote teams. You can use it using any of your device with Slack. Pointing a story is as easy as typing /pointstory your story title In a slack channel.
